My objective is to bridge the gap between analytics and creativity. With academic foundations in psychology, chemistry, and data science, I aim to use analytical thinking to better understand human behavior and the ways we interact with art, games, music, and storytelling.

I believe data and creativity are not opposites but collaborators, and they enrich each other. Whether I'm building a model, making music, or designing a game, my goal is the same: to create meaningful connections between people and ideas through insight, emotion, and shared experience.
